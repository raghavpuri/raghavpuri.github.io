Most Recently, I

- did a Fellowship at 8VC and met some cool people.
- built software for a drone system to map biodiversity in rainforests for the Rainforest XPrize (we got eliminated in the quarter finals :( )
- suffered the consequences of taking too many STEM classes at Brown.

A little less recently, I
- set up a supply-chain for manufacturing low cost wearables for Alzheimers patients in New Delhi.

- I interned at a startup based in New Delhi to develop a decentralised system for emergency medical record retrieval in case of accidents to pitch to the Government of India.

All of this may sound impressive by traditional measures. Know, though, that this is - while being true - also a very incomplete picture of me.
